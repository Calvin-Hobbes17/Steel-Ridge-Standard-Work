# Plate Design

Plates are one of the most important structural elements on a robot.

## Lightening

Pocketed and Lightened Parts not only are lighter, but they also look much nicer than aluminum sheet.
To make Pocketed Parts, we use team 2471's Part Lighten Featurescript. For a guide on how to use this featurescript check out FRCDesign.
